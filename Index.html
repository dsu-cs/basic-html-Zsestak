The Perfect Grilled Cheese

How To Make the Perfect Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2–3 slices of cheese
  • 1–2 tablespoons of softened butter

    • Preparation

    Spread butter on the bread.

    Then Place the cheese between the slices with the butter on the outside.

    Cooking

    Place the sandwich in the pan and cook for 3–4 minutes until the bottom is golden brown. Flip, and cook for another 2–3 minutes until the second side is browned and the cheese is fully melted.

    Then cut and let rest for a few minutes before eating.
